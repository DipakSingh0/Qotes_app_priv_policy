Privacy Policy for Quotes App

Last Updated: [October 21, 2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the Quotes App mobile application.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Quotes App is built with your privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe that an app designed to inspire should also respect your personal space and digital privacy. Therefore, we want to be absolutely clear:

1. No Data Collection Whatsoever

Quotes App operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, transmit, or process any information from its users or their devices.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or unique device identifiers.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app. There is no logging of which quotes you view, how often you use the app, or your interactions with any features.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect any information about your device, such as your IP address, operating system, device model, or any other technical data.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, crash reporting services) that would gather information about your app usage or device.
  • No Internet Connection Required: The App functions completely offline once installed. It does not initiate any outgoing internet connections to transmit data. All functionality is contained locally within the app.
  • No Session Persistence: Since no data is saved beyond the current session, there are no user profiles, settings, or history stored locally or remotely. Each use of the app is private and self-contained within your device.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use your information for any purpose, nor do we share it with any third parties. Your use of Quotes App is private, anonymous, and entirely contained within your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Quotes App does not integrate with any third-party services (such as advertising networks, social media SDKs, cloud storage, or analytics providers) that would collect or process user data.

4. Permissions Used by the App

Quotes App requires minimal permissions to function. The app may request notification permissions to alert you of the Quote of the Day or other content, but this operates entirely locally on your device. We do not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive data on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Quotes App does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13, as it does not collect any information at all. The content of this app is suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
